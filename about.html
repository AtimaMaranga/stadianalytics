About Stadi Research

Transforming research and analytics across East Africa

Our Story

Stadi Research & Analytics Ltd was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to bridge the gap between data and decisions across East Africa's academic and business communities.

Based in Nairobi, Kenya, we have grown from a small consultancy to a trusted partner for over 50 organizations and hundreds of students. Our dual focus on academic excellence and corporate intelligence sets us apart in the research consulting landscape.

We believe that quality research should be accessible to everyone – from the master's student working on their thesis to the multinational corporation planning their East African expansion strategy.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and organizations with high-quality research and analytics services that drive informed decision-making and sustainable growth across East Africa.

Our Vision

To be East Africa's leading research and analytics consultancy, recognized for excellence, innovation, and positive impact on academic and business communities.
